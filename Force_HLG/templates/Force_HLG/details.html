In this module you will learn how to solve force problems using Newton's Second Law.

First you will solve a few problems just to get warmed up.
Next you will watch a few short videos. There will be questions at the end of each section.
It is okay to go back and re-watch parts of the video to help you answer the questions.
Finally you will take a short quiz at the end based on the material from the module

Our Learning Goals Will Be

To resolve a vector into its components
To write an equation for the net force
To write Newton's second law
To solve Newton's second law

Mind Wandering Definition and Prompts

At some point, you may realize that you are no longer thinking about the current task (i.e., learning physics), but you may be thinking about something else altogether. This is called zoning out.
Prompts will frequently pop up that ask if you are feeling zoned out.
Please be as honest as possible about reporting zoning out. It is perfectly natural to zone out while reading.
